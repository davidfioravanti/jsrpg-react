WHY DO I NEED AN ACCOUNT TO PLAY?

In order to save your progress across play sessions, JSRPG needs some way to keep track of your progress. You wouldn't want to lose all the time spent delving dungeons! In order to do this, JSRPG requires a quick and painless sign-in (none of that "confirm your email address before you can continue" crap).
